Get ready to experience an unforgettable night of music and magic deep within the historic Château Neercanne in Maastricht. On the 28th of October, the castle's ancient caves will come alive with the beats of renowned DJ Chris Stussy and a lineup of his talented friends.

Nestled in the picturesque landscape of Maastricht, Château Neercanne is a stunning 17th-century castle known for its unique caves, which have been transformed into a mystical party venue. As you step inside these underground chambers, you'll be transported to a world of enchantment, where the fusion of music and history creates an ambiance like no other.

Chris Stussy, a maestro of electronic music, will take center stage, curating an electrifying atmosphere that will keep you dancing throughout the night. With the support of his talented friends, the party promises an eclectic mix of beats and rhythms that will leave you mesmerized.

As the night unfolds within the atmospheric caverns of Château Neercanne, you'll have the chance to explore the castle's rich history, adding a touch of mystique to your party experience. Whether you're a seasoned electronic music enthusiast or just looking for a unique adventure, this party promises to be an exceptional journey into the heart of Maastricht's cultural and musical scene.

Mark your calendars for the 28th of October and prepare to join Chris Stussy & friends for a night of music, dance, and enchantment inside the captivating caves of Château Neercanne. This is a party you won't want to miss, where history and beats unite in perfect harmony.
